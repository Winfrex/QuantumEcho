Pricing & Licensing

Quantum Echo is licensed to match the scale of your project — from small independent games to enterprise systems. Simple monthly tiers for developers and flat, predictable options for commercial or OEM integration.

Game Development Licensing

For developers and small studios, Quantum Echo’s game license is designed to grow with your success. Pay a low monthly fee during development, scale down after release, and royalties only begin once your title earns real revenue.

Phase Cost When It Applies Notes
Development $79 / month per project (IP) During active development Includes SDK, dashboard, and updates for up to two active developers.
Post-Release $39 / month per project After launch and under $100K lifetime revenue Maintenance license for continued updates and runtime support.
Success Tier 5%–3% royalty (monthly fee waived) Once revenue exceeds $100K Royalties scale down as total revenue grows. See breakdown below.
Royalty Breakdown (After $100K Revenue)
$100K – $500K 5% — Matches Unreal’s standard rate.
$500K – $1M 4% — Reduced rate for moderate success.
$1M – $5M 3.5% — Reward for sustained performance.
Over $5M 3% — Maximum reduction for large-scale hits.

Royalty Justification: The structure matches industry norms at lower tiers and rewards long-term success. Monthly fees stop once royalties begin, ensuring predictable and fair costs at every stage of growth.

Commercial & Industrial Licensing

For industries using Quantum Echo as an infrastructure component — not a game runtime — flat or node-based licensing provides predictable cost and simple deployment.

Industry Model Typical Starting Price
CAD / Engineering Per-seat or per-project license From $500 / seat / month
Financial / Trading Per-node or per-server annual license From $1,000 / node / month
Video / Rendering Per-seat or render-node license From $300 / node / month

*Flat or node-based licensing applies to all non-game use cases. Contact us for integration or cluster pricing.*
